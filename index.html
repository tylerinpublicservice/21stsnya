About the 21st Cohort

A youth-centered platform for representation, service, dialogue, and national participation.

Learn More

The 21st Cohort of the Seychelles National Youth Assembly brings together young leaders committed to civic responsibility, parliamentary engagement, and constructive public service. It offers a space where youth voices can contribute to national conversation and community development.

Through committees, assembly business, outreach, and public initiatives, the cohort supports informed participation and practical action during the 2026-2027 mandate.
