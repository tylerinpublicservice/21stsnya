About the 21st Cohort

The 21st Cohort of the Seychelles National Youth Assembly represents a new generation of youth leadership committed to participation, dialogue, and national development. Through civic engagement, committee work, and parliamentary-style proceedings, the cohort provides a platform for young people to contribute meaningfully to issues of national importance.

The 2026-2027 mandate focuses on representation, service, advocacy, and youth-led action across a broad range of areas affecting communities and the future of Seychelles.
